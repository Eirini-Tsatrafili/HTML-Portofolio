The Best Marvel Movies according to Eirinaki!

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Avengers: Endgame (April 26, 2019)

One of the most amazing marvel movies, with an incredible plot and the most insane battle ever seen in the movie theaters.

Spider-Man: No Way Home (December 17, 2021)

If you are a marvel fan you are going to love this one. Incedible plot, highly unpredictable and , of cource, very funny and handsome protagonists.

Guardians of the Galaxy (August 1, 2014)

One of the most heartwarming and funny marvel movies. Watch how a group of weird individuals becomes a family while saving the universe.
